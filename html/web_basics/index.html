Otters

Otters are like cats, but they live in the water. They're water cats! Some people think seals are water dogs, but otters are water cats.

Just like cats they are:

  • playful
  • adorable

There are many types of otters, but the two I've most often heard referred to are:

  1. river otters (there is more than one type of river otter)
  2. sea otters
